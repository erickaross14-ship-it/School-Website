About Our Kindergarten

We provide a safe, stimulating and caring environment for children to learn and grow.

We Make Learning Fun!

At Sunshine Kids Academy, we believe that early childhood education should be a joyful adventure. Our dedicated teachers create engaging activities that spark curiosity and foster a love for learning.

Our curriculum is designed to develop the whole child - socially, emotionally, physically, and intellectually - through play-based learning and hands-on experiences.

Our Programs

Age-appropriate classes designed to nurture your child's development

Language Activities

We help learners build strong communication skills through listening, speaking, reading, and early writing. Children enjoy songs, stories, poems, and role-play that encourage confidence and expression.

Mathematical Activities

Through games and practical experiences, children learn to count, compare, sort, and solve simple problems. They build early numeracy and logical thinking skills that prepare them for primary learning.
